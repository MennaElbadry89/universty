Research at KFUPM

At KFUPM, our research focuses on addressing major global challenges. We measure our impact against the United Nations' 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to ensure our work contributes meaningful solutions.

By nurturing new sectors through impactful research, we drive economic diversification and make a tangible impact for a brighter future. We are doing this through collaboration at all levels, from more than 20 research centers to creating research relationships with government and the private sector. Our commitment to collaboration is what sets us apart. Here are some ways we are doing this:

We research

We invent

We collaborate
