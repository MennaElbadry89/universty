About Us

We’ve come a long way from our humble beginnings in 1963 to support the burgeoning oil industry in Saudi Arabia. Today, KFUPM is much more than a leading energy institution. We are the first choice in future-sector industries, such as quantum computing, artificial intelligence, bioengineering, and video game design technologies.

Just as Saudi Arabia is undergoing a remarkable transformation, so too has KFUPM evolved far beyond its roots in the energy industry. Today, we pioneer advances in future-focused fields, preparing students to lead in a rapidly changing world. And while we embrace innovation, we remain dedicated to providing a vibrant, supportive, and safe campus environment where students can thrive academically and personally.

Student life at KFUPM is vibrant, academically fulfilling, and safe. We enhance well-being by making sure every student has opportunities to access a secure campus with plenty sporting activities, quality accommodations, recreation facilities, events, healthcare.
